About Fixed Gear Bikes

Fixed gear bikes, also known as fixies, have a single gear and no freewheel mechanism. This unique design gives riders a direct connection to the bike, offering simplicity and control.

Fixie Tips

  • Always check your brakes and tires before riding.
  • Practice stopping safely using your legs and brakes.
  • Join a crew for group rides and knowledge sharing!
